SmartBCH ready tools

Here are some tools, known to work with SmartBCH mainnet and testnet blockchains.

Wallets

  • Metamask - standalone iOS and Android applications and browser extension
  • InstaWallet - instant burner wallets on mainnet
  • Testnet InstaWallet - instant burner wallets on testnet

Block Explorers

  • SmartScan - supports both mainnet and testnet blockchains

Faucets

  • SmartBCH testnet faucet - sending 0.1 sBCH

Exchanges

  • BenSwap - The very first DEX on SmartBCH
  • PONY NFT Marketplace - AI generated ponies
  • Web3 NFT Shop and Minting Service
  • AMKT Easy NFT Builder - 1-Click Mint on SmartBCH and NFT collections

Other tools

  • JustSmartContracts - "bookmark" certain EVM smart contracts and then watch for their properties and events. Works with SmartBCH (allows custom RPCs)

You can also view some known SEP20 tokens on smartBCH.
